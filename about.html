About Me

Junior Web Developer & Problem Solver

Hello, I'm Jonathan Stewart!

I'm a junior web developer with a real passion for turning ideas into clean, responsive, and user-friendly websites. What excites me most is creating digital experiences that not only look great but actually make people's lives easier and more enjoyable.

I specialize in front-end development and have built a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I love crafting beautiful, intuitive interfaces and bringing designs to life with smooth interactions.

My journey into coding began at Oakland Community College. What started as curiosity quickly turned into a passion — I was hooked by the magic of writing code and instantly seeing a website come to life. That blend of creativity and problem-solving keeps me motivated every day.

I'm particularly drawn to turning complex problems into simple, elegant solutions while keeping performance, accessibility, and clean code in mind. Right now, my goal is to grow as a confident front-end developer by mastering modern tools like React and Tailwind CSS, gaining real-world experience, and contributing to meaningful projects.
